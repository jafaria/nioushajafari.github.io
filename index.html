Niousha Jafari

Where I'm coming from

I was born in Iran and received my first computer (Windows 98) when I was nine. I fell in love with computers since then. When I was 11, we moved to Prague, Czech Republic where I received my first laptop, a Macbook Pro! I took my first programming class in high school and decided that's what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.

Where I'm going

I'm constantly amazed by the power of technology and how many new things are out there to learn. During each project I learn a lot about different languages and coding skills. I'd like to continue that and work on more great projects that teach me to think differently.
