I was born in Iran and received my first computer (Windows 98) when I was nine. I fell in love with computers since then. When I was 11 my family moved to Prague, Czech Republic where I received my first laptop: a Macbook Pro! I took my first programming class in high school and decided that it's what I want to do for the rest of my life.

I am constantly amazed by the power of technology and how many new things are out there to learn. During each project I learn a lot about different languages and coding skills. I'd like to continue that and work on more great projects that teach me to think differently. More specifically, I am interested in front-end development and Android Mobile Apps.
