Iran News Bot
Source code

After Iran was the topic of every news headline, I wanted to see the different angles each news source paid attention to in the same story. The bot reteweets "Iran" related news to show the difference in 140 characters or less.
The project could further improve by enhancing the filter on tweets to include more relevant tweets, using NLP.
